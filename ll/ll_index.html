How It Works

While we most of our clients come to us to maintain their stringed instruments, our true passion is making custom guitars and basses. Whether you're looking for an acoustic to play by the campfire, an electric guitar to shred, or a Flamenco guitar for a change of pace, we've got you covered.

After you place your order, one of our luthiers will review all the details with you to ensure you'll getting exactly what you want before we start creating your instrument.

Once we've begun the build process, you can keep track of its status using our online progress tracker. We'll even post pictures along the way, so you can see your instrument take shape before your eyes!

And for all of you southpaws out there, we have you covered. Our master luthier knows the pain of dealing with limited selection all too well, so every instrument we make can be made for a righty or lefty.

Finally, we'll tune, set-up, and restring any instrument we make for free for the lifetime of the instrument, and provide a discount on repairs should it be damaged.
